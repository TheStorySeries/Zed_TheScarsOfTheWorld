Scene - 1 [In an Old Police Quarter]

Fredrick (Yelling) : Come on. Let's get together and get into this truck. We have to leave now. Take the guns with you.

Johnson : Yes, let's leave, Fredrick. All the people who managed to get into this truck will come with us.

Fredrick is a tall and muscular man with short brown hair and a beard. He wears a police uniform, which is torn and bloodstained. He has a gun hanging from his waist and a police radio on his belt. He is the leader of this group of survivors

Fredrick : All right, let's go.

Johnson : Check all the ones who got into the truck; make sure nobody is missing.

Johnson Hamilton is a 38-year-old man who is an energetic and brave person but could also be very rude and a bit impatient. He appears to be a fine-built, muscular man, black-haired and blue-eyed. Besides serving on a medical team in the military, he also worked as a medic in the field, so he had to venture into some of the most dangerous areas in search of the wounded. Over time, he had learned to take care of his injuries and had become a highlyskilled combat medic. At the age of 37, he resigned from the military to take care of his family.

Someone in the Truck : Yes, sir. I checked all the people who came into the truck. Nobody is missing. We should leave this place as soon as possible.

Fredrick : Where is Patrick, Johnson? I don't see him.

Johnson : Patrick !!!

Patrick : I'm here, Uncle Johnson.

Patrick is a tall, well-balanced, black-haired boy with curly black hair and brown eyes. He is a teenager who loves music a lot, and he also had a good personality in high school. (After completing high school, he worked as a waiter in a hotel named Imperial to pay for his knowledge and for his rented house where he lived.

Fredrick : We have to stay protected or else they will attack us. We should vacate this place as soon as possible

Johnson : We should go to another place. It will be safer there. Ok, where do you suggest we go, Sir?

Fredrick : There is another place which is much safer than this...

Scene - 2

A rainy place. Suddenly a voice, a deep voice with a cold tone.

Unknown Man Voice : We can't stay here for more time. Move on.

Unknown Childish Voice : Okay, Father.

We hear a conversation between a son and his father. The son is named Piper Davis, and the father is named Nelson Davis.

Nelson : Don't get into the danger. If the blood comes from your body. They will smell us and will kill us

Piper : I understand Father.

In the middle of the forest, it started to rain heavily. The rain was so dense that it became hard to see. In the middle of this forest, they saw a way which connected to a road and hoped that someone would rescue them in case.

Nelson : Oh, my leg...

Piper : What happened, Father?

Nelson : No, nothing. You just run towards the road. I will follow you.

Nelson was injured in his leg, and his leg began to spasm. Suddenly, he heard a quite loud growling sound, which was getting closer to him and getting louder. Piper shouts in the distance.

Piper : Father, I found a car.

Inside the Car

Piper : Come on, Wake up !!

(Piper slapped the driver while nudging him awake. He woke up startled, then looked at Piper.

Driver : Hey, what's wrong with you?

Piper (Crying): Please help me, Uncle. Something chasing us.

Driver : Us ?

Piper : Myself and my Father !

Driver : Where is he now ?

Nelson : I am coming, Son. Open the door.

Suddenly, something caught the leg of Nelson and dragged him inside the forest. Many zombies ran toward him.

Nelson (In Pain) : You leave this place Son !!

Piper : I don't want to leave, Dad.

The driver and Piper watched with horror as Nelson was eaten alive by the horde of zombies. They couldn't do anything to save him. Piper started to cry hysterically.

Driver : We need to leave this place now.

The car was moving very fast, but the zombies were still able to keep up. One of them jumped onto the top of the car and started to try and bite the driver.

Driver :Just listen. Control the steering, Boy

Piper : Okay, Sir

The car accelerated at a high speed, but the zombies was still able to keep up. The driver leaned his head outside of the window and shot the zombie that was on top of the car. It fell from the car and did not move anymore. He controlled the car and then, hours later, the zombies were far away from them.

Piper : What's your Name ?

Driver : I am Peter Wilson. What's Yours ?

Piper : I am Piper Davis.

Peter : What is your father's name?

Piper (Sadly) : My father's name was Nelson. I should have saved him.

Peter : : I know this might sound strange, but don't worry about your father. He tried everything he could to save you. And you did everything you could to save him. It's just the way things are.
